Salos

Seafront boutique development of only 44 units in a breathtaking location. This project is designed to maximize sea and mountain views and offers a perfect getaway in the Mediterranean.

With 2 beautiful lagoons in a walking distance, gym, sauna, and swimming pool on the site, this is THE place for those who are looking to enjoy their stay with idyllic views and maximum privacy.

At the same time, maintained sandy beach, mountain walking trails, equipped picnic areas, various restaurants, and local shops are only a short 5 minutes’ drive away, creating a perfect blend of comfort, tranquility, and relaxation.

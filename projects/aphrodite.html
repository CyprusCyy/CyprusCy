Aphrodite Park Residence

Located on the sandy beaches on the west coast of Cyprus, Aphrodite Park Residence offers an incredible seaside living experience. Seafront dining, rooftop pool and bar, gym and spa.

The fourth phase Aphrodite Beachfront Project is a development of Studio, 1 and 2 bedroom apartments all with direct sea views just 400m from the sea and adjacent to the Aphrodite 1, 2 and Aphrodite SPA Residence projects.

The area of Gaziveren on the West Coast of North Cyprus offers a relaxed lifestyle, with easy access to towns and amenities yet only one hour from Ercan International Airport.

About CyprusCy

CyprusCy is a Cypriot based company run by experienced property consultants from Norway, Cyprus, Lithuania, Poland, Ukraine, United Kingdom, Iran, Lebanon, Syria, and Dubai.

We are proud to have exclusive rights to prestige projects throughout the island in collaboration with developers, who specialize in modern villas and apartments with unique design, quality, and location.

Each project has its own character, limited availability, and aims to provide the highest level of lifestyle whilst preserving the natural environment. We can offer extended repayment plans, and on some projects, financing may be available.

A home purchase abroad is a big step that requires careful planning, and an accomplished property consultant who can guide you throughout the buying process.

If you choose to buy without a consultant, there is a higher risk involved. We have a diverse team which covers all aspects of the processes.

The team at CyprusCy understands how challenging and confusing it can be moving or investing into a new country. We spend time to discuss the values of our clients, so that we can customize everything to you.
